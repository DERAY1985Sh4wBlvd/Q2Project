Phineas and Ferb

This tells a story about 2 young children as the 2 main protagonists who always come up with wonderful ideas to do during their 104 days of summer vacation before school comes to an end it. They spend their fun days with their families and friends while the best spy, Perry The Platypus, has his own fun with Dr. Doofenshmirtz, which is basically fighting.



Who are the characters of Phineas and Ferb?

  • He is the person that generates wonderful ideas so that everyone can have fun. Futhermore, he is extremely intelligent, creative, and is purposeful.
  • The Stepbrother of Phineas Flynn
